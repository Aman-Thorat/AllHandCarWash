Frequently Asked Questions

What steps should I take to prepare my vehicle before entering the car wash?

To prepare your vehicle before entering the car wash, follow these steps to ensure a safe and effective car wash experience. First, remove any loose or valuable items from the interior of your vehicle to prevent damage or loss during the wash. Next, ensure all windows and the sunroof are fully closed to keep water out of the cabin. Fold in side mirrors to prevent damage, and check roof-mounted accessories like luggage racks to make sure they are securely fastened. Turn off your windshield wipers to avoid damage to the blades, and clear any extended antennas. Address any leaks in your vehicle, such as from sunroof seals, before the car wash. Pay attention to specific car wash instructions or signage, and choose the appropriate wash option for your vehicle's needs. Follow gear selection guidelines, whether it's putting your vehicle in neutral or park, as specified by the car wash facility. These steps will help ensure a smooth and safe car wash experience.
